Welcome to Homomorphic Encryption Demo

Homomorphic encryption is a form of encryption that allows computations to be performed on encrypted data without decrypting it. This provides a high level of security and privacy for sensitive data processing.

This demo showcases the concept of homomorphic encryption by performing addition and multiplication operations on encrypted data and obtaining the results.
